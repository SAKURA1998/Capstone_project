Detailed Description

It doesn't allocate any internal buffers and needs less than 1 kb of memory. One compressor instance needs just 5x CPU compared to a SuperpoweredFilter.

Parameters
inputGainDbInput gain in decibels, limited between -24.0f and 24.0f. Default: 0.
outputGainDbOutput gain in decibels, limited between -24.0f and 24.0f. Default: 0.
wetDry/wet ratio, limited between 0.0f (completely dry) and 1.0f (completely wet). Default: 1.0f.
attackSecAttack in seconds (not milliseconds!). Limited between 0.0001f and 0.03f. Default: 0.003f (3 ms).
releaseSecRelease in seconds (not milliseconds!). Limited between 0.1f and 1.6f. Default: 0.3f (300 ms).
ratioRatio, rounded to 1.5f, 2.0f, 3.0f, 4.0f, 5.0f or 10.0f. Default: 3.0f.
thresholdDbThreshold in decibels, limited between 0.0f and -40.0f. Default: 0.
hpCutOffHzKey highpass filter frequency, limited between 1.0f and 10000.0f. Default: 1.0f.
