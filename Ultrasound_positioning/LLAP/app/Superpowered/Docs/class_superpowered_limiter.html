Detailed Description

It doesn't allocate any internal buffers and needs less than 1 kb of memory. One limiter instance needs just 2.5x CPU compared to a SuperpoweredFilter.

Parameters
ceilingDbCeiling in decibels, limited between 0.0f and -40.0f. Default: 0.
thresholdDbThreshold in decibels, limited between 0.0f and -40.0f. Default: 0.
releaseSecRelease in seconds (not milliseconds!). Limited between 0.1f and 1.6f. Default: 0.05f (50 ms).
